Power¶

acoustics.power.lw_iso3746(LpAi, LpAiB, S, alpha, surfaces)[source]¶

Calculate sound power level according to ISO 3746:2010.

Parameters:
  • LpAi (np.ndarray) – Sound pressure levels of the source .
  • LpAiB (np.ndarray) – Background noise sound pressure levels .
  • S (float) – Area in square meters of the measurement surface .
  • alpha (np.ndarray) – Absorption coefficients of the room .
  • surfaces (np.ndarray) – Room surfaces.
Returns:

Sound power level .
